Thank you to everyone who attended PhoneGap Day EU and made it such a huge success. Here's the next highlight video for those of you who weren't able to attend.

Our next video is from Ian Jorgensen who intrigued the audience with his title "What do Phonegap, Canvas and Urine have in Common?". It turns out that the answer is "Quite a lot!". Ian participated in Health Hackday in Stockholm which is a 24-hour competition to create an app related to healthcare. Ian's talk covers how they used PhoneGap to create Piddle, an app that helps people with urine analysis.
